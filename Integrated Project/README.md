
# 🚀 From Data to Decision: An Integrated Growth System

> *Most projects touch one function. This one was designed to show what happens when all five work together.*

---

## 🏢 Business Context

Most organisations optimise functions in isolation — the data team builds dashboards, the strategy team makes plans, operations executes, marketing broadcasts, and technology runs automations. Each function does its job. The system still underperforms.

This project was built on a single hypothesis: **if you fix the data layer first, every downstream function gets smarter automatically.**

---

## ❓ Business Problem

A growth-stage retail business had data, but no single view of it. Strategy decisions were made on instinct. Operations ran on generic sequencing. Marketing sent the same message to every customer. Reporting was done manually every week. The result was a company working hard in five different directions at once.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Design and build a cross-functional operating system where data, strategy, operations, marketing, and AI feed each other — demonstrating that integrated analysis produces outcomes no single-function approach can match.

---

## 🔧 Approach & Methodology — Five Disciplines, One System

### 📊 Data Layer
Built a unified dataset tracking customer behaviour, revenue, and operational metrics in a single structured view. Eliminated siloed reporting by centralising all KPIs into one source of truth.

- Structured data model covering customers, transactions, product performance, and operational metrics
- Data cleaning and normalisation to ensure consistent definitions across functions
- Single source of truth that all downstream analysis draws from

---

### 🧠 Strategy
Used the unified data to identify the top 20% of customers generating 80% of revenue. Built a retention-first strategic roadmap around that segment rather than chasing broad acquisition.

- Pareto analysis to isolate high-value customer segment
- Retention roadmap with prioritised touchpoints for top-tier customers
- Competitive positioning logic based on behavioural data, not assumptions

---

### ⚙️ Operations
Redesigned fulfilment sequencing to prioritise high-value orders, eliminating the bottleneck where premium customers waited behind low-margin bulk orders.

- Order prioritisation logic built into the operational workflow
- Fulfilment sequencing redesigned around customer value tier, not order timestamp
- Result: average delivery time reduced by **22%**

---

### 📣 Marketing
Used customer segmentation data to shift from broadcast messaging to personalised outreach — different content for different behavioural segments.

- Behavioural segmentation applied across customer base
- Content strategy differentiated by segment: high-value retention vs. mid-tier activation vs. lapsed re-engagement
- Result: engagement rose **35% within 6 weeks**

---

### 🤖 AI & Automation
Automated the weekly reporting cycle and anomaly detection layer using AI workflows, removing the manual reporting burden from the analytics team entirely.

- Automated weekly performance report generation
- Anomaly detection flagging unusual revenue or inventory movements in real time
- Result: **8 analyst hours saved per week** (400+ hours per year returned to strategic work)

---
